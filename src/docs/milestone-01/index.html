Application Parts

The existing plans page is necessary to understand the function of the application. A plan is a group of trails, flights, and hotels that the user puts together to create a trip. The existing plans page stores all existing plans and allows users to edit or delete their plans. Next to each plan is the estimated length of each trip. This page offers an organized space for all plans so that users can easily see what they have coming up or what plans they can put into action when they only have a certain amount of time on their hands.

The recommendation page is one of our most important pages as it caters to newer users. It recommends trails based on the information the user provides. The user chooses their experience level, their desired trail distance, and a general location and our app makes API calls to hiking trail APIs to get trails that fall within the user's specifications. Users can then add this trail to an existing or new plan. This feature encourages users to hike as it eliminates the discouraging process of sifting through trails that may be out of their comfort zone.

The search page is another key component of our application. Users are greeted with different categories of what to search for such as trails, hotels, and flights. Once they click on their preferred category, they can search within that category, and corresponding results show up with descriptive images and an option to add that result to a current or new plan. The search page allows users who already have an idea of what they would like to do on their trip the freedom to search without messing with small details present on the recommendation page.
